About Me!

Krista P.

Illustrator & Grahpic/Web Designer

Hello! I am Krista, otherwise known as Jynxx-Chan on my socials.
I am a self taught illustrator and I am currently taking graphic design courses. I am a proud cat mom of three, with a big love for animals and nature. I have been interested in the arts for as long as I can remember. I started my journey redrawing Ed Hardy tattoos you could get from the little coin machines at the grocery store. After that I really got into anime which has led my art to where it is now.
